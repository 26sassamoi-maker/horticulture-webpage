What is Horticulture?

Horticulture is the science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants. It combines biology, environmental science, and hands-on cultivation to create healthy, thriving plant life. This webpage explores plant types, care techniques, and the beauty of horticulture.

Types of Plants

  • 🌿 **Herbs** – Basil, mint, rosemary
  • 🌸 **Flowers** – Roses, tulips, sunflowers
  • 🌳 **Trees** – Oak, maple, birch
  • 🥕 **Vegetables** – Carrots, tomatoes, lettuce
  • 🍎 **Fruits** – Apples, strawberries, grapes

Basic Plant Care Tips

  • 💧 Water consistently, but avoid overwatering
  • ☀️ Ensure proper sunlight based on plant type
  • 🌱 Use nutrient-rich soil
  • ✂️ Prune dead leaves to encourage growth
  • 🪴 Repot plants when they outgrow their containers
